Procrastination?

Time management. As a student we were given an activity we should finish. And it is our responsibility to finish it on time. But sometimes, I feel unmotivated to work. As it leads to procrastination. So I would find something that could keep me motivated. For example, after I finish everything, I will play online games with my friends. Or other things that could give me energy like music, art, and reading stories. I could also make the due date my inspiration. "I have to finish this because the due date is near". I would always find something that could keep me going. Or Set schedules and to-do lists.
