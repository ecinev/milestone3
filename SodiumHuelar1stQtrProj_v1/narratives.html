Cruel is Beeautiful

"Shhh" He shushed the little girl crying in his arms. "Don't cry brat" Not hearing the man's words, she kept crying in agony as she hid at the man's embrace, "They got murdered" she said in her tremulous voice, traumatized at the scene earlier. A 7 year old girl was seen at the huge manor crying alone seeing her parents getting murdered in a brutal manner before her eyes while her siblings was taken away. Hiding in the closet feeling helpless. Such a traumatic scar that she will have to endure for a lifetime. The family was envied by many, how good looking, how smart, how rich, such nobles living in a faraway forest, where peace exists, where warmth and love was shared. But are mistaken by many, alot thought that they are such greedy people, but how can an innocent peaceful family can be seen so violent? I guess people, assume too much or rather they are too creative with their own ideas. The pleas of the little young girl are still alive at this man's heart. He couldn't even imagine if he was in her situation, children like her should be playing doing things they want to do, but I guess the world is too cruel for a young innocent girl, A world where you can experience satisfaction yet you can experience hunger, a world where you experience warmth yet can experience cold, a world where cruel is beautiful....

I love rainy days

I love rainy days The Pouring of the rain. The sound it makes when it touches the ground. And the cold wind. Sitting beside the open window. As tiny droplets of water. Somehow made its way through. Despite its coldness. I feel warmth. I feel comfort I feel full. The coldness was never enough Listening to the harmonious music that nature makes. As it plays a pretty tune. Looking up above. You can clearly see. How those dark clouds move at a fast pace. The smell. Remains a nostalgic impression. Now, the rain is dying down. Whilst watching everything from the start. Till the end. I still could never get enough. The rainy days. Of yesterday. So Peaceful. Felt Blissful. I wish that it can continue. Raining. For eternity.
